### Lectură obligatorie

To prevent unwanted updates, we will disable an option made by Sony which automatically download the update file from their servers on your vita.
### Ce aveți nevoie

* O conexiune la Internet pe PS Vita (TV)

### Instrucțiuni

#### Section I - Disabling the auto-download option


1. Navigați spre `System` -> `Auto-Start Settings`
1. Debifaţi "Download Update File for System Software"
1. Reveniți la meniul principal de Settings
